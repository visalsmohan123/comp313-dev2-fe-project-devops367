Eligibility for Blood Donation

Basic Criteria

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • Your weight should be at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • You should be in good health and feeling well on the day of donation.

Additional Considerations

  • Recent travel to certain countries may affect eligibility.
  • Some medical conditions or medications may disqualify you temporarily.
  • Check if you've donated blood in the last 8 weeks.

Please consult a healthcare professional if you have any doubts about your eligibility.
